# User Guide — Creating Your BEO

> Your biology. Your data. Your protocol.

---

## What is a BEO?

A **Biological Entity Object (BEO)** is your permanent, sovereign biological identity.

Unlike a traditional health record that belongs to a hospital or laboratory, your BEO belongs to **you**. You hold the private key. No company — including the Ambrósio Institute — can access your data without your explicit consent.

Your BEO exists permanently on the Arweave blockchain. It cannot be deleted by anyone.

---

## Creating Your BEO — 4 Phases (~12 minutes)

The onboarding is intentionally designed to take time. You are setting up a biological identity for the next 80 years. That deserves a moment of attention.

### Phase 1 — Identity (~3 min)

1. Open any BSP-compatible app
2. Choose your `.bsp` domain — your permanent biological address
   - `firstname.bsp` (e.g. `andre.bsp`)
   - `initials-year.bsp` (e.g. `am1985.bsp`) — more private
   - A random identifier — maximum privacy
3. Your private key is generated on your device. It **never leaves your device**.
4. Your BEO is registered on Arweave. This cannot be undone.

### Phase 2 — Sovereignty (~4 min)

Set up your **guardian network** — 3 trusted people who can help you recover your BEO if you ever lose your device.

- You need **2 of 3 guardians** to confirm your identity for recovery
- No single guardian can access your data
- No central server is involved

Also: write down your 24-word seed phrase and store it offline. This is your last resort if guardians are unavailable.

### Phase 3 — Activation (~3 min)

Add your first biological data:
- **Import from a BSP-Certified lab** — automatic
- **Connect a wearable device** — Oura, Apple Watch, WHOOP, Garmin
- **Enter manually** — values from a recent blood test

### Phase 4 — Living Protocol (~2 min)

Authorize AVA (optional) to analyze your BioRecords and generate your first Ambrósio Vitality Score (SVA) — your multi-dimensional biological age.

## FAQ

**What if I lose my phone?**
Use your guardian network (2 of 3 guardians confirm your identity) or your seed phrase.

**Can the Ambrósio Institute read my data?**
No. The Institute holds no keys. Your private key never leaves your device. The Institute can only analyze data you explicitly send for AVA processing — and only when you initiate it.

**What if the Ambrósio Institute closes down?**
Your BEO and BioRecords remain on Arweave permanently. They are not on Ambrósio servers. The protocol continues to exist regardless of what happens to any company.

**Can I have more than one BEO?**
Each person is designed to have one BEO — your lifelong biological identity. Multiple BEOs for the same person defeat the purpose of a unified biological history.
