# Complete Biomarker Taxonomy

The BSP Taxonomy is the universal language for measuring biological state. Version 0.2 contains **210+ approved biomarkers** across **25 categories** and **4 access levels**.

> [!NOTE]
> This is a living standard. New biomarkers are added quarterly via the [BIP Governance Process](/governance).

## Taxonomy Overview

| Level | Name | Categories | Biomarkers | Certification Required |
|-------|------|-----------|-----------|----------------------|
| **L1 — Core** | Longevity & Aging | 9 | ~45 | BSP-2 Advanced or higher |
| **L2 — Standard** | Clinical Laboratory | 8 | ~85 | BSP-1 Basic |
| **L3 — Extended** | Specialized & Research | 7 | ~65 | BSP-2 Advanced or BSP-3 Full |
| **L4 — Device** | Continuous Monitoring | 1 | ~15 | BSP-4 Device |

## Level 1 — Core Longevity Biomarkers

Advanced longevity and biological aging markers. The scientific foundation of the BSP — biomarkers that directly measure mechanisms of aging. Required for BSP-2 Advanced certification and above.

### 1. Longevity & Aging — `BSP-LA`

*GDF-11 emerged from parabiosis research at Stanford and Harvard as a circulating rejuvenation factor. Telomere length is validated in meta-analyses of >100,000 participants as a predictor of all-cause mortality.*

| Code | Marker | Description |
|------|--------|-------------|
| `BSP-LA-001` | **GDF-11** | Growth Differentiation Factor 11 — circulating rejuvenation signal; declines with aging and correlates with regenerative capacity loss |
| `BSP-LA-002` | **TIMP2** | Tissue Inhibitor of Metalloproteinase 2 — neural aging inhibitor identified in young plasma at Stanford as a cognitive restoration factor |
| `BSP-LA-003` | **TELO** | Telomere Length — direct cellular aging indicator; validated predictor of all-cause mortality (>100,000 participants) |
| `BSP-LA-004` | **NAD+** | Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide — metabolic energy and DNA repair; declines ~50% between ages 40–60, impairing sirtuin function |
| `BSP-LA-005` | **MMP9** | Matrix Metalloproteinase 9 — cellular inflammation and extracellular matrix remodeling; marker of tissue aging and chronic inflammatory burden |

### 2. Regeneration & Cellular Health — `BSP-RC`

*HOMA-IR is the most epidemiologically validated metabolic longevity predictor. mTOR is the master regulator of autophagy — the cellular self-cleaning process.*

| Code | Marker | Description |
|------|--------|-------------|
| `BSP-RC-001` | **HOMA-IR** | Insulin Resistance Index — most validated metabolic longevity predictor; accelerates aging in all tissues when chronically elevated |
| `BSP-RC-002` | **IGF-1** | Insulin-like Growth Factor 1 — cellular regeneration and growth; optimal range associated with reduced chronic inflammation (IJMS, 2025) |
| `BSP-RC-003` | **MTOR** | mTOR Pathway Activity — master regulator of autophagy; determines cellular capacity to eliminate damaged components |
| `BSP-RC-004` | **IL-6** | Interleukin-6 — primary upstream inflammatory signal; drives CRP synthesis in the liver and predicts mortality in longitudinal cohorts |

### 3. Cardiovascular Health — `BSP-CV`

*ApoB is endorsed by international cardiology consensus as the most accurate cardiovascular risk marker. Each atherogenic particle carries exactly one ApoB molecule.*

| Code | Marker | Description |
|------|--------|-------------|
| `BSP-CV-001` | **APOB** | Apolipoprotein B — endorsed by cardiology consensus as superior to LDL; directly counts atherogenic particles regardless of size |
| `BSP-CV-002` | **HCIS** | Homocysteine — independent cardiovascular and neurodegenerative risk marker; reflects methylation pathway dysfunction |
| `BSP-CV-003` | **NITOX** | Endothelial Nitric Oxide — primary vascular aging signal; decline indicates arterial stiffness and endothelial dysfunction |
| `BSP-CV-004` | **OMEGA3** | Omega-3 Index (EPA/DHA) — anti-inflammatory cardiovascular biomarker; low levels independently associated with arrhythmia and mortality |

### 4. Immune Function & Inflammation — `BSP-IM`

*Chronic low-grade inflammation (inflammaging) is the common denominator of all aging-related diseases. A 2025 AJCN RCT demonstrated daily Vitamin D3 reduces biological aging equivalent to ~3 years.*

| Code | Marker | Description |
|------|--------|-------------|
| `BSP-IM-001` | **VITD** | Vitamin D (25-OH D3) — 2025 RCT demonstrates supplementation reduces biological aging equivalent to ~3 years; broad immune regulatory function |
| `BSP-IM-002` | **GSH** | Total Glutathione — primary systemic antioxidant; decline indicates compromised cellular defense against oxidative damage |
| `BSP-IM-003` | **hsCRP** | High-Sensitivity CRP — detects low-grade chronic inflammation below standard CRP threshold; preferred marker for longevity risk stratification |
| `BSP-IM-004` | **CD38** | CD38 Expression — NAD+-consuming enzyme; elevated in immunological aging, creating a self-reinforcing cycle of energy depletion |

### 5. Metabolism & Cellular Energy — `BSP-ME`

*Metabolic health is the foundation of functional longevity. Mitochondrial ATP production is the most direct marker of cellular energy function.*

| Code | Marker | Description |
|------|--------|-------------|
| `BSP-ME-001` | **ATP** | Mitochondrial ATP Production — direct measure of cellular energy efficiency; qualitative dysfunction detectable even with preserved mitochondrial quantity |
| `BSP-ME-002` | **BHB** | Beta-Hydroxybutyrate (Ketones) — marker of metabolic flexibility; capacity to efficiently access multiple fuel sources |
| `BSP-ME-003` | **LACT** | Resting Plasma Lactate — indicator of chronic metabolic acidification and impaired mitochondrial efficiency at baseline |
| `BSP-ME-004` | **GLUC** | Fasting Glucose — universally validated metabolic marker; chronic elevation accelerates aging through glycation of proteins |

### 6. Neurological Health & Cerebral Clearance — `BSP-NR`

*The 2025 Nature Medicine study established brain biological age as the strongest predictor of 15-year healthspan. Research in Brain (Oxford, 2025) confirmed glymphatic-lymphatic clearance is measurable in living humans.*

| Code | Marker | Description |
|------|--------|-------------|
| `BSP-NR-001` | **BDNF** | Brain-Derived Neurotrophic Factor — primary marker of neuroplasticity; governs synapse formation and neuronal survival |
| `BSP-NR-002` | **CORT** | Circadian Cortisol Rhythm — regulator of sleep-wake cycle and immune function; chronic dysregulation correlates with accelerated epigenetic aging |
| `BSP-NR-003` | **AMPB** | Plasma Beta-Amyloid — blood-based marker of cerebral clearance; elevation precedes plaque deposition by years |
| `BSP-NR-004` | **TAUPHOS** | Phosphorylated Tau (p-Tau) — marker of cerebral clearance dysfunction; detectable in blood as early signal of neurological aging |

### 7. Detoxification & Hepatic Health — `BSP-DH`

*The liver is the primary detoxification organ. GGT has been identified as an independent predictor of cardiovascular mortality beyond liver function.*

| Code | Marker | Description |
|------|--------|-------------|
| `BSP-DH-001` | **GSHRED** | Hepatic Reduced Glutathione — active antioxidant form in liver tissue; measures hepatic detoxification capacity (distinct from systemic GSH in BSP-IM) |
| `BSP-DH-002` | **ALT** | Alanine Aminotransferase — clinical gold standard for hepatocellular damage; detects liver compromise before clinical disease |
| `BSP-DH-003` | **GGT** | Gamma-Glutamyl Transferase — sensitive to xenobiotics and hepatic damage; independent predictor of cardiovascular mortality |
| `BSP-DH-004` | **HGMET** | Heavy Metals Panel (Hg, Pb, As, Al) — sub-clinical accumulation impairs mitochondrial function, immune response, and hormonal signaling |

### 8. Lymphatic System & Systemic Clearance — `BSP-LF`

*Research in Nature Communications (2024) demonstrated that ablation of meningeal lymphatic vessels doubled beta-amyloid retained in the brain. JEM (2023) linked aging CD4/CD8 T cells to meningeal lymphatic dysfunction.*

| Code | Marker | Description |
|------|--------|-------------|
| `BSP-LF-001` | **LYMP** | Lymphocyte Count & Morphology — primary indicator of lymphatic immune health |
| `BSP-LF-002` | **CD4CD8** | CD4/CD8 Ratio — JEM (2023): elevated IFN-γ from aged T cells directly impairs meningeal lymphatic drainage; actionable marker of lymphatic aging |
| `BSP-LF-003` | **VEGFC** | VEGF-C — primary lymphangiogenic factor; Nature Communications (2024) demonstrated restoration of meningeal drainage and improved amyloid clearance in aged models |
| `BSP-LF-004` | **LDH** | Lactate Dehydrogenase — marker of lymphatic congestion and cellular damage |

### 9. Biological Clock & Cellular Senescence — `BSP-BC`

*The most advanced category in the BSP taxonomy. GDF-15 is the most promising clinical proxy for epigenetic aging clocks. DunedinPACE measures the rate of aging rather than accumulated age.*

| Code | Marker | Description |
|------|--------|-------------|
| `BSP-BC-001` | **GDF-15** | Growth Differentiation Factor 15 — validated proxy for GrimAge and PhenoAge epigenetic clocks; more practical than DNA methylation sequencing (Biogerontology, 2024) |
| `BSP-BC-002` | **KLOTHO** | Soluble Klotho — longevity regulator modulating multiple aging hallmarks simultaneously; declines progressively with biological aging (JGG, 2025) |
| `BSP-BC-003` | **P16** | p16/CDKN2A — direct marker of cellular senescence burden; senescent cells secrete SASP factors that accelerate tissue aging |
| `BSP-BC-004` | **PACE** | DunedinPACE — measures rate of biological aging rather than accumulated age; responsive to short-term interventions |
| `BSP-BC-005` | **PAI1** | Plasminogen Activator Inhibitor-1 — GrimAge component; strongly associated with vascular aging and all-cause mortality |
| `BSP-BC-006` | **FGF21** | Fibroblast Growth Factor 21 — emerging marker of metabolic stress, mitochondrial function, and systemic aging trajectory |
